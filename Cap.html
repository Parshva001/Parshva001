    • Captain America

    Steve Rogers
    Alias: Captain America, Cap
    Played by: Chris Evans
    Background: Born in the ‘20s, Rogers was The First Avenger. He gained powers from a ‘Super Soldier Serum’ and was frozen in time, waking in the modern day.
    Superpowers: Super strength, speed and agility; good morals and leadership ability
    Fun fact: Captain America Comics #1 is one of the most valuable comics, with a near-mint issue estimated at $115,000. Will we see him again, and where? Yes, he’s already confirmed for Joss Whedon’s upcoming The Avengers: Age Of Ultron (2015), and will likely get a third solo film (Chris Evans is not retiring from acting after all).
