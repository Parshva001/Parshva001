    • THE MARVEL CINEMATIC UNIVERSE

    As the name suggests, the Marvel Cinematic Universe is a shared universe that Marvel Comics superheroes exist in, on film. Which movies are in the universe? Captain America: The First Avenger and Captain America: The Winter Soldier; The Iron Man movies; The Avengers, Avengers: Age of Ultron, Avengers: Infinity War, and Avengers: Endgame, just to name a few. The first Marvel movie to win any Oscars was the popular film Black Panther. In February of 2019, it won three Academy Awards: Best Costume Design, Best Production Design, and Best Original Score. Some of the actors that portray these legendary characters are Chadwick Boseman as Black Panther; Tom Holland as Spider-Man; Robert Downey Jr. as Iron Man; Brie Larson as Captain Marvel; and Natalie Portman as Mighty Thor.
